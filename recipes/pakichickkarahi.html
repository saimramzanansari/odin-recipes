Pakistani Chicken Karahi

Recipe to make a spicy and flavorful Pakistani chicken karahi.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add chicken pieces. Cook until golden brown.
  2. Add onion and cook until translucent.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook until they break down.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and spices. Cook for a minute.
  5. Add salt and cook for another minute.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with naan or rice.
